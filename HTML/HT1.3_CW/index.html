Festivals

Diwali

Diwali, the Festival of Lights, is one of India's most celebrated festivals.

Holi

Holi, the Festival of Colors, is known for its vibrant celebrations.

Eid

Eid is a significant muslim festival marked by prayers and feasting.

Places to visit

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Jaipur

Jaipur, often referred to as the Pink City, is known for its historic sites, including

  • Amer Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace

Kerala

Kerala is fomous for its serene backwaters and lush green landscapes.

Indian Cuisine

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a creamy and flavorful Indian dish.

Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant and aromatic rice dish with vatious regional variations. Notable ingredients include:

  1. Basmati rice
  2. Chicken or lamb (vegetables for a vegetarian version)
  3. Spices (cumin, coriander and garam masala)
  4. Yoghurt and saffrom for flavor and color

Dosa

Dosa is a south Indian crispy pancake made with fermented rice and lentil batter.
