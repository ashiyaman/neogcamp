Early Life

William Henry Gates III was born on October 28, 1955, in Seattle, Washington. He showed an early interest in computers and programming, creating his first program at the age of 13.

Co-Founding Microsoft

In 1975, along with his friend Paul Allen, he co-founded Microsoft, a company that would play a significant role in the personal computer revolution. Microsoft's operating system, MS-DOS, became the foundation for their success.

Software Innovations

Under Gates' leadership, Microsoft developed and released Windows. Microsoft and Gates launched their first retail version of Microsoft Windows on November 20, 1985,The sales of Windows made Microsoft a major player in the industry.

Philanthrophy

In 2000, Gates and his wife combined three family foundations and donated stock valued at $5 billion to create the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.The goals of the foundation are to enhance healthcare and reduce extreme poverty across the world, and to expand educational opportunities and access to information technology in the U.S.

Post-Microsoft

Since leaving day-to-day operations at Microsoft, Gates has continued his philanthropy and works on other projects. On February 4, 2014, Gates stepped down as chairman of Microsoft to become technology advisor at the firm to support newly appointed CEO Satya Nadella.
