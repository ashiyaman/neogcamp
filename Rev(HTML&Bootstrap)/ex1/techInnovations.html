Microsoft Windows

One of Bill Gates' most influential contribution is the development of Windows. Microsoft and Gates launched their first retail version of Microsoft Windows on November 20, 1985, which had captivated consumers with its simplicity and ease of use.

Microsoft Office Suite

Gates played a pivotal role in the creation of Microsoft Office Suite, which includes essential productivity tools such as Microsoft Word, Excel and Powerpoint. These applications became industry standards for word processing, spreadsheet management and presentations.

Internet Explorer

Microsoft's Internet Explorer launched in 1995, was one of the early web browsers that played a significant role in popularising the internet.
