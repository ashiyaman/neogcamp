Travel Tips

Travel Light

Only pack essentials to make your journey more comfortable. Check the weather of your destination and pack accordingly.

Plan your itenary

OPlan your trip well in advance to make the most of your time at the destination.

Stay hyderated

Travelling can be dehydrating. So drink plenty of water.

Keep important documents safely

Make photocopies of important documents like passport, ID and other documents. Keep them in separate place from the originals in case of theft.
