Interesting facts about Mumbai

Dabbawalas

If you get on any local station, you can find these Dabbawallas waiting to make their delivery to provide, homemade meals to people who were going to offices. With an accuracy of 99.67%, they have achieved 6 stigma standards without using s a single piece of technology. This ideal delivery system has even had case studies done on by the prestigious Harvard Business School.

Mahalaxmi Dhobi Ghat

The Mahalaxmi Dhobi Ghat at Mumbai is the largest open-air laundry in the world where countless laundrymen from across the city come to wash clothes. More than 8000 laundrymen can be seen at work at any point in time, washing away at the 700 stone washing platforms. It also won the Guinness Book of Awards for "most people hand-washing clothes simultaneously at a single location" in 2011.

First-Ever Indian Train

Mumbai was the place where the train was started. The first train was announced and opened for public in the year 1853 and it has become a major source of public transportation from that time.
