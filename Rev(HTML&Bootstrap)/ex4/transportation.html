Transportation in Mumbai

Local Trains

Mumbai's lifeline, the local train network, is one of the busiest in the world connecting the metropolitan city.

BEST Buses

The Brihanmumbai Electric Supply and Transport(BEST) buses provide a extensive transportation network within the city.

Metro

Mumbai Metro provides connectivity with eastern and western part of the city.

Taxis and Autos

Widely available for point-to-point commutes.
