Explore the Vibrancy of Mumbai City

Mumbai (formerly known as Bombay) is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. Mumbai is the financial capital and the most populous city of India.

Mumbai is the financial, commercial, and entertainment capital of South Asia.

Interesting facts about Mumbai

  • Initially, Mumbai was made up of 7 different islands – Colaba, Mazagaon, Little Colaba, Parel, Mahim, Isle of Bombay, and Worli.
  • The dabbawallas are Mumbai’s pride. It was formed in order to provide, homemade meals to people who were going to offices.
  • India’s 1st Five-star hotel is the Taj Mahal Palace which is located in Colaba, Mumbai.
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus(CST) is an UNESCO World Heritage sight and a prominent railway station.
  • Mumbai's railway network is one of the busient in the world, connecting millions of commuters daily.

Transportation of Mumbai

  • Local Trains: The Mumbai Suburban Railway, popularly referred to as Locals forms the backbone of the city's transport system.
  • BEST Buses: Public buses run by BEST cover almost all parts of the metropolis
  • Metro Rail: Mumbai Metro provides connectivity with eastern and western part of the city.
  • Monorail: The Mumbai Monorail, opened in February 2014, is the only operational monorail system in India and also is the seventh largest Monorail system in the world.
  • Taxis and Autos: Widely available for point-to-point commutes.
