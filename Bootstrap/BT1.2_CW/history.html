History

The Taj Mahal was built between 1631 and 1648 and is the result of the profound love Emperor Shah Jahan had for his wife Mumtaj Mahal. Mumtaj Mahal died during childbirth. It took over 20,000 workers and artisans to complete and artisans to complete this magnificent structure.

The Taj Mahal is a testamemt to the artistry of Mughal architecture and has a rich history, including surviving wars and natural disasters.
